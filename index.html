Size: 4.7 feet (width) x 14 feet (length) A well-designed size that ensures spaciousness for passengers while retaining maneuverability in varied terrains.

Motor Controller: 5 kW Equipped with a high-efficiency 5 kW motor controller for optimal power delivery, ensuring excellent performance and reliability. Top Speed: 35-45 km/hr A balanced speed range that provides a safe and controlled driving experience on all types of terrain.

Hydraulic Brakes on All Four Wheels Advanced hydraulic braking system for effective braking power on all wheels, providing maximum safety during drives. Hand Brake Additional hand brake for extra stability when parking or stationary, especially on inclines.
4. Differential & Suspension:
Axle Differential: Graziano (German Made) Fitted with a Graziano axle differential, which ensures efficient torque distribution and high performance on off-road surfaces, delivering superior reliability.
5. Wheels & Tires:
Tyres: 15-inch Heavy Mud Proof Tires Durable 15-inch mud proof tires designed to handle rough terrain and provide maximum traction, stability, and safety in off-road conditions.
6. Dashboard & Infotainment:
Digital Speedometer A modern digital speedometer that offers accurate speed tracking, providing a user-friendly interface. Infotainment System: 9-inch Android Display Featuring a 9-inch Android car player, the infotainment system supports music, navigation, and more, ensuring a comfortable and enjoyable ride.
7. Seating & Roof:
Detachable Roof The roof can be easily detached, allowing an open-air experience for enjoying scenic rides. Foldable Seat Adjustment Features foldable seat adjustments for customized comfort and increased versatility, accommodating passengers and cargo conveniently.
8. Charging & Battery:
Charging Time: 4-5 Hours Equipped with an efficient charging system that fully charges the Jeep in just 4-5 hours, ensuring minimal downtime between trips.
9. Additional Accessories:
Toolkit Comes with a handy toolkit for routine maintenance and quick repairs during your journey. Extra Color for Touch Up Includes extra paint for touch-ups, keeping your EV Rubicon Jeep looking new and well-maintained.

High-Performance German Components: The Graziano axle differential provides excellent reliability and power distribution, tailored for off-road performance. Comfort & Convenience: The detachable roof and foldable seat adjustment allow for versatile configurations, adapting to passenger and cargo needs. Advanced Safety: Equipped with hydraulic brakes on all four wheels and a hand brake, ensuring maximum safety during your rides.
