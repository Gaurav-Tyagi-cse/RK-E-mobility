Classic Rebuild and fabrication

RK Emobility proudly showcases its exceptional expertise in the restoration and fabrication of golf carts, delivering comprehensive solutions that revitalize any branded golf cart or vintage buggy, no matter its condition.
Our restoration journey begins with a thorough initial inspection, followed by the convenient collection of your vehicle directly from your location using our dedicated transport service. Once your cart arrives at our advanced facility in Vasai Mumbai Maharashtra or any of our certified RK Emobility workshops, our experienced technicians conduct a detailed assessment to determine the necessary enhancements.
In cases where the motor controller is beyond repair, RK Emobility has the expertise to install new driving motor systems, ensuring your golf cart operates at peak performance. Our restoration services encompass not only mechanical fixes but also aesthetic upgrades. We specialize in rejuvenating body components, repairing damaged parts, and providing complete color customization tailored to your specifications.
With RK Emobility, the transformation process is remarkable; clients are often astonished by how their rejuvenated vehicles look and perform. Our commitment to quality and our skilled craftsmanship position RK Emobility as the leading choice for golf cart restorations and fabrications in India.
